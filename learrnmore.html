About Recycling and Sustainability

Why Recycling Matters

Recycling helps reduce waste, conserve natural resources, and protect the environment. By properly recycling items, we can decrease pollution and create a cleaner, healthier world for future generations.

How to Recycle Properly

Follow these simple steps to recycle effectively:

  • Separate recyclables from regular trash.
  • Clean containers to remove food residue.
  • Check your local recycling guidelines for specific instructions.
